The Best Celebrity Halloween Costumes of 2022

May 13, 2024
blog

Harry Styles' Halloween Costume

As part of "Harryween" on Oct. 31 at the Kia Forum in Los Angeles, Harry Styles transformed into his best Danny Zuko as his band dressed as different characters from Grease. The theme didn't just stop at the costumes, Styles also performed a rendition of "Hopelessly Devoted to You."

Kendall Jenner's Halloween Costume

For one of Kendall Jenner's Halloween costumes this year, the model poked fun at herself in a giant cucumber costume.

The outfit was a nod at her viral moment where she awkwardly cut a cucumber in an episode of The Kardashians.

For the costume, she accessorized with a fake kitchen knife, green Spandex leggings and bright green eyeshadow. She captioned an Instagram image of herself in the costume, "I'm giving out fruits and veggies tonight."

Lizzo's Halloween Costume

Lizzo also opted for more than one costume this year. This time, she went as The Muppets' Miss Piggy sporting ears, a pig nose and a blonde wig. In one clip, she even put on her best Miss Piggy voice.

Alessandra Ambrosio's Halloween Costume

Alessandra Ambrosio hosted Darren Dzienciol's Annual CARN*EVIL Halloween Party this year and to do so, she dressed to the nines. The model wore a black leotard covered in chains and was draped in a black cape. To top off the look, she wore light-colored contacts in her eyes and a headpiece.

Anderson .Paak's Halloween Costume

Anderson .Paak transformed into a nun on Oct. 29 while spinning as his alter ego DJ Pee .Wee at the opening of Starchild Rooftop Bar & Lounge at CIVILIAN Hotel in New York City.

Justin Timberlake and Jessica Biel's Halloween Costumes

For Halloween this year, Justin Timberlake and Jessica Biel dressed as "The Wet Bandits," Harry and Marv, in a nod to the classic holiday film, Home Alone.

"Harry, it's our calling card! All the great ones leave their marks," the couple quoted from the 1990 Christmas comedy alongside their Instagram photo.

"We're the wet bandits!" they added.

Becky G's Halloween Costume

For Darren Dzienciol's annual CARN*EVIL Halloween Party, Becky G dressed as Santanico Pandemonium from 1996's From Dusk Till Dawn.

Sam Richardson's Halloween Costume

Sam Richardson gave a nod to Mattel with his Halloween costume this year. The actor rocked a head-to-toe look that included a chest harness, skull mask and staff as he transformed into the character Skeletor from the Masters of the Universe franchise.

Machine Gun Kelly and Megan Fox's Halloween Costumes

Megan Fox and Machine Gun Kelly showed off one more costume before the clock officially struck on Halloween. The two transformed into Nintendo's Link and Zelda on Oct. 31 for another spooky soirée.

Brad Goreski's Halloween Costume

When looking for inspiration for his Halloween costume this year, Brad Goreski looked no further than a Housewife wedding. The TV personality dressed as Teresa Giudice from her wedding to Luis Ruelas this summer. Complete with the exaggerated up-do and tiara, Goreski transformed into "Teresa Giudeski."

Lil Nas X's Halloween Costume

Lil Nas X did not stop at just one costume this year. For a party at Delilah in L.A., the rapper showed off his abs in this look which featured gold horns, gloves and shoulder armor.

Jaden Smith's Halloween Costume

For Halloween this year, Jaden Smith opted for Harry Potter. Posing in front of a rather Hogwarts-like backdrop, Smith shared a photo wearing a Gryffindor robe and wizard's iconic eyeglasses.

Cardi B's Halloween Costume

Cardi B took a trip to Springfield this year for Halloween! Dressed as Marge Simpson, the rapper sported the character's iconic blue hair, red beaded necklace and even painted her body yellow.

Pink's Halloween Costume

Pink's costume was a blast from the past as she dressed as the children's storybook character Raggedy Ann. The singer shared a photo to Instagram wearing a red yarn wig and the doll's signature blue, red and white dress.

Chace Crawford's Halloween Costume

In a post shared to the social media platform over the weekend, the model showed off her Catwoman costume posed alongside her own caped crusader. While we can't much of his face, Kerr's companion is likely husband Evan Spiegel.
