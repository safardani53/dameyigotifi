Kate del Castillo Negrete Trillo is an American-Mexican actress. At the age of 19, del Castillo was known to have starred in telenovela Muchachitas for Televisa in 1991. After that, she continued her film and television career in Latin America, playing in telenovelas, including Alguna vez tensremos. Alas, La Mentira, Ramona, and Bajo la misma piel. In 2011, del Castillo gained worldwide recognition playing in the Telemundo La Reina del Sur series. In 2017, she starred in the Netflix political drama Ingobernable as Mexico's First Lady, Emilia Urquiza. del Castillo made her Hollywood debut as a female lead in the 2007 television series Under the Same Moon and later appeared in supporting roles in the films No Good Deed, The 33, El Chicano and Bad Boys for Life .
