After a photo of Lauren "Lolo" Wood hit social media, pregnancy speculation persisted online. Now, the model has confirmed that she's expecting.

Living life in the public eye comes with its share of pros and cons. While celebrities' popularity increases, their privacy can become non-existent — especially when it comes to their personal lives. With the pregnancy rumor mill always buzzing, every day another female celebrity is believed to be expecting a child. And Lauren “Lolo” Wood is no exception.

Lauren and Cleveland Browns player Odell Beckham Jr. first went Instagram official in November 2019. Since then, the former Wild ‘N Out girl and fitness trainer has been posting adorable snaps of her beau on social media.

After a certain photo surfaced online, pregnancy rumors went into overdrive for the couple. And now, it appears that Lolo has responded to the chatter. So, is Lolo Wood pregnant? Keep reading to find out.

Lolo Wood has confirmed that she is expecting her first child with Odell Beckham Jr.

Oh baby! On Nov. 22, 2021, Lolo confirmed the news many fans expected — she is in fact pregnant with her first child! The model shared the news with fans and supporters via Instagram with a slideshow of herself and Odell posing for photos with her gorgeous bump on full display.

"I love you, I love you, I love you," Lolo captioned the photo.

Odell enthusiastically commented, “Can’t wait, baby!”

As expected, fans and fellow celebrities alike have flocked to Lolo's Instagram to congratulate the couple on their bundle of the joy.

"Yes congratulations beautiful," Nazanin Mandi commented. "OMG CONGRATS. So happy for you guys," supermodel Chanel Iman commented.

Social media users initially believed that Lolo Wood was expecting after a photo surfaced in October 2021.

In October 2021, a photo of Lolo working out in the gym started to circulate online. At first, some fans believed it was nothing out of the ordinary since she works as a fitness trainer, but for others, the appearance of her stomach apparently told another story.

In the image, which was reposted by OnSite! on Oct. 5, 2021, Lolo can be seen standing in a blue fitness suit. And fans were quick to comment that it looks like her stomach area is protruding. As expected, social media users were pretty vocal with their opinions.

“Ugh I feel bad, women should be able to announce on their own time, congrats though,” one user commented.

Fans believe that Lolo Wood may have privately confirmed her pregnancy back in September 2021.

Internet sleuths are convinced that Lolo may have previously confirmed her pregnancy. An Instagram account, @cloutbound_, shared the gym photo during the week of Sept. 26, 2021.

Since Lolo was a former Wild 'N Out girl and works as a model, fans expect for the beauty to have a sizable net worth. And her net worth is likely steadily growing.
