Actress Emma Roberts Has A Multimillion Dollar Net Worth

May 10, 2024
blog

Emma Roberts began her career in Hollywood at the early age of nine. She has starred in successful TV and movie projects, including American Horror Story and Nancy Drew.

Roberts is a well-paid and sought-after actress who has earned a huge net worth from endorsement deals and commercial work in addition to her acting career.

How rich is Emma Roberts? A look at her early career

Roberts has amassed an impressive net worth of $25 million as of 2023, according to Celebrity Net Worth, thanks to her successful acting career. 

She made her debut in the acting world at the age of nine, landing a role in Ted Demme’s 2001 drama film Blow, portraying Kristina Jung, the daughter of Johnny Depp’s character. 

This marked the beginning of a career that has seen her take on diverse roles in films such as the family film Grand Champion and the Canadian adventure movie Spymate. 

Emma Roberts. (Source: Instagram)

In 2004, the actress transitioned to television and gained widespread recognition for her lead role as Addie Singer in the Nickelodeon sitcom Unfabulous, earning several award nominations.

Beyond acting, Roberts delved into the music industry, releasing her debut album titled Unfabulous and More in 2005. 

Her filmography continued to expand with roles in movies like Aquamarine, for which she won a Young Artist Award, and the title role in Nancy Drew. 

Despite a brief foray into music, Roberts expressed a preference for focusing on her acting career. Notable films included Wild Child, Lymelife, Hotel for Dogs, and The Winning Season.

Emma Roberts’ mature roles

In 2010, Roberts appeared in multiple films, including Valentine’s Day, Twelve, Memoirs of a Teenage Amnesiac, 4.3.2.1., It’s Kind of a Funny Story, and Virginia. 

The following year, she starred in the romantic comedy The Art of Getting By alongside Freddie Highmore and played the role of Jill Roberts in Wes Craven’s Scream 4. 

She continued to diversify her roles in the 2012 film Celeste and Jesse Forever, parodying pop stars like Kesha, and portraying Riley Banks, an incurious blonde singer. 

In 2013, she starred in the comedy-drama film Adult World, receiving praise for her ad-libbed and genuine performance. 

Roberts also made her mark in television with her participation in FX’s American Horror Story series, starting with Coven and continuing with Freak Show, Cult, Apocalypse, and 1984.

Moreover, the 32-year-old actress made her return to American Horror Story for Delicate as the main role of Anna Victoria Alcott alongside Kim Kardashian.

Exploring Emma Roberts’ real estate

In 2010, Roberts invested $1.25 million in a 2,124 square-foot home featuring two bedrooms, two and a half baths, and an open-plan living area with a terrace and eat-in kitchen. The property includes an attached two-bedroom guesthouse with a fitness area and recording studio.

In 2018, the actress acquired a stunning Mediterranean-style residence in the Laughlin Park community of Los Feliz, Los Angeles, for $4.02 million. The 3,802 square-foot home boasts three bedrooms, two full bathrooms, and two half bathrooms. 

The main-floor living spaces feature walnut wood floors and custom mahogany built-ins, including a skylight-topped entrance gallery, a living room with a carved-stone fireplace, and French doors leading to an awning-covered terrace. 

The dining room showcases floor-to-ceiling French doors opening to a balcony, while the well-equipped kitchen boasts granite countertops on mahogany cabinets and high-end designer appliances. 

The bottom-level family room, with travertine tile floors and a walk-in wet bar, opens to a lush yard with a swimming pool, a separate circular spa, an outdoor kitchen and bar area, and terraced gardens with stone pathways. 

In 2020, Roberts sold a Los Feliz home for just under $6 million. In April 2022, she expanded her real estate holdings with a $3.6 million purchase of a Hollywood Hills mansion that belonged to British actress Minnie Driver. 

Emma Roberts has endorsed several renowned brands

Roberts’ first big endorsement came from skincare giant Neutrogena, proving her worth as a marketable figure in the beauty business in addition to her flexibility as an actress. 

The Scream Queens star lent her face to multiple TV ads for Neutrogena, solidifying her presence in the world of skincare. 

Beyond Neutrogena, she extended her influence to various brands. Her portfolio includes commercials for renowned brands such as BOSS, Aerie Real, Fiat, and Verb. 

Emma Roberts’ net worth will rise in the upcoming years

Roberts’ net worth will grow in the upcoming years, as she is busy with numerous projects under her belt. 

In March 2019, she was announced as the star of the romantic comedy film Holidate, released in October 2020. She also ventured into the controversial thriller genre with her role in the 2020 film The Hunt. 

Adding to her filmography, Roberts was cast in the romantic comedy About Fate and produced the thriller film Abandoned alongside John Gallagher Jr. and Michael Shannon. 

Fast Forward to 2023, she appeared in Michael Jacobs’ romantic comedy movie Maybe I do as Michelle and will appear in the 2024 movie Madame Web which is currently in post-production.

Beyond acting, she showcased her multifaceted talents by voicing the character Sasha Nutwagon in the animated film Saurus City, currently in post-production. 

In addition, her executive producer role in the forthcoming film Space Cadet is indicative of her growing influence behind the scenes.

Emma Roberts’ early life

Roberts, born on February 10, 1991, in Rhinebeck, New York, is the daughter of Kelly Cunningham and renowned actor Eric Roberts. Unfortunately, her parents’ marriage ended in divorce when she was just seven months old. 

Through her father’s subsequent marriage, she gained a stepmother, Eliza Roberts, and step-grandparents, David Rayfiel and Lila Garrett. 

On her mother’s side, she became the stepdaughter of musician Kelly Nickels. Roberts has a younger maternal half-sister named Grace. 

Her paternal grandmother was the esteemed acting coach Betty Lou Bredemus, and her aunts include the iconic actresses Julia Roberts and Lisa Roberts Gillan. 

Growing up, Roberts had the opportunity to spend time on the sets of her aunt Julia’s films, igniting a passion for the film industry and inspiring her to follow in the footsteps of her father and aunts.

Speaking of her ethnicity, she is of diverse European descent, with roots tracing back to England, Scotland, Ireland, Wales, Germany, and Sweden through her father’s heritage.
