Who is Andra Day and is she married?

July 10, 2024
blog

SINGER and actress Andra Day stars as the legendary jazz singer Billie Holiday in the new Hulu film United States vs Billie Holiday.

Day will star in her first film after making an appearance in the Disney Pixar film Cars 3.

Who is Andra Day?

Born December 30, 1984, Cassandra Monique Batie, better known by her stage name Andra Day, is a singer, songwriter and actress from San Diego, California.

At a young age, Day was introduced to the sounds of jazz vocalists Billie Holiday, Ella Fitzgerald and Dinah Washington, all of whom had an influence on her sound.

In 2015, Day, 36, released her debut album Cheers To The Fall, which peaked at number 48 on the US Billboard 200 chart.

The 36-year-old was nominated for Best R&B Album in the 2016 Grammy Awards, as well as Best R&B Performance for her main single Rise Up.

Day appeared alongside Stevie Wonder, who is partially credited for her discovery, in an ad for Apple TV in 2015.

She received two nominations at the Golden Globe Awards for Best Actress in a Motion Picture - Drama and Best Original Song for her work as Billie Holiday in the Hulu film United States vs Billie Holiday.

Is she married?

Day is single, she's never publicly revealed anything about her romantic life.

According to Whodatedwho, Day was in a relationship with chef Don Bowie in 2015.

However, the actress never confirmed or denied those rumors.

What TV and movies has she starred in?

Day has appeared in three film throughout her acting career.

She played a small nightclub singer role in the 2017 film Marshal, which starred late actor Chadwick Boseman.

Day was the voice of Sweet Tea in the Disney Pixar movie Cars 3.
