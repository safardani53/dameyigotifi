Who is Dr Guddi Singh?

Dr Guddi Singh is a Paediatrician and TV broadcaster.

She is an Integrated Child Health Quality Improvement Fellow and a Paediatric Registrar at East London NHS Foundation Trust.

She is also on the Executive Management Team of the Centre for Health and the Public Interest.

Dr Singh has a Masters in Public Health from Harvard University.

She has previously worked for the World Health Organization.

Dr Singh is active on Twitter under the username @DrGuddiSingh.
