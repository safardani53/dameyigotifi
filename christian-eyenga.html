Career

Christian Eyenga has been playing professional basketball since 2006 and continues to do so. He began playing professionally before being drafted by the Cleveland Cavaliers in the 2009 NBA Draft. Prior to the 2010 NBA draft, he played for Onatra Kinshasa and DKV Joventut.

On January 2, he made his Cavaliers debut against the Dallas Mavericks, scoring 4 points and grabbing 3 rebounds. He joined Joventut Badalona in Spain during the 2011 NBA lockout in October 2011. He was moved to the Los Angeles Lakers on March 15, 2012, but was subsequently transferred to the Orlando Magic on August 10, 2012.

To play in China, he was released by the Legends and re-joined upon his return from China. In China, he represented the Shanxi Zhongyu. He went to Poland on July 25, 2013, to join Stelmet Zielona Gora, then he signed with another Polish club, Turow Zgorzelec, in September 2014. On November 12, 2014, he signed a one-year deal with the Italian Series A side Pallacanestro Varese. On July 10, 2015, he signed with Dinamo Sassari for the 2015-16 seasons before joining Auxilium CUS Torino for the remainder of the season. He re-signed with Varese for the 2016-17 season on August 16, 2016.
