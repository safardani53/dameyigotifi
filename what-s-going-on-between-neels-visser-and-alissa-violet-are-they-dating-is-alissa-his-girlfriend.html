What's going on between Neels Visser and Alissa Violet? Are they dating? Is Alissa his girlfriend?

Neels Visser and Alissa Violet have not even crossed 20 years of age, and they already have millions of fans worldwide. Though both of them are pursuing modeling as their career, they are mostly known for their online personas. Instagram, Twitter, Vine, Facebook, and Snapchat are only a few online platforms that they have sparkled on.

Neels is from Arizona, whereas Alissa was born in Ohio. Though hailing from two very different states in the US, they met in California. They met for the first time around the end of 2015 in Los Angeles. Both of them were working on their careers out of LA at that point. And since then, they have been inseparable. 

Alissa called Neels her BFF in an interview.

In an interview on March 2016 on the red carpet at the " held at The Beverly Hilton Hotel in Los Angeles, California, Alissa and Neels shared a candid chat.

In all of their public appearances and public photo sharing, both Neels and Alissa seem very comfortable with each other.

But both of them have openly denied having an affair with each other. And they did not even show a bit of acceptance of the possibility that they could be dating in the near future. Neels denied Alissa was his girlfriend. But it is still not clear if they had ever been in a relationship. They could have had a breakup and they could have just slyly dodged the question by cunningly talking of only the present status.

However, fans of Alissa and Neels ship this pairing a bit too much. An Instagram page has been created in which fans post the duo's moments together. The following Instagram post shows Alissa trying to get a hold of Neels hand and Neel jerking his hand away.

So, does this mean Alissa is the one pursuing and Neels' the one trying to avoid?
