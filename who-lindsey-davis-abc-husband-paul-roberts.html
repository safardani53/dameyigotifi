ABC NEWS journalist Linsey Davis is a familiar face on World News Tonight and Good Morning America.

Away from the cameras, the 51-year-old is a wife and mother of one.

Who is Linsey Davis' husband, Paul Roberts?

Linsey Davis and her husband, Paul Roberts, tied the knot on March 10, 2013.

The ceremony was held at the Oheka Castle in New York.

While not much is known about Paul's personal life, as per Linsey's posts on Instagram, the couple enjoys running marathons together for charity and spending quality time with family.

In a number of her social media posts, Linsey refers to Paul as her "better half" and one Valentine's Day 2021, she posted a selfie of the two together, captioned: "Here is to love... here is to life... here is to you ❤️"

Do Linsey Davis and Paul Roberts have kids?

Linsey and Paul share one child together. Their son, Ayden, was born in 2014 and is the inspiration behind her fourth publication, How High is Heaven?

The 2022 children’s book was written to help her son, and other children, find peace and understanding in losing a loved one. Ayden was only one year old when he lost his paternal grandmother, Grandma P.

"I know the idea that they'd be reunited at some point, gave [Ayden] some solace," Davis told People.

"And that's kind of my goal for kids today, especially kids who are losing their parents and grandparents to COVID, and for other reasons as well, that kids will have some amount of consolation that there is hope that they'll see their loved one again."

Who is Linsey Davis?

Linsey Davis is an American broadcaster, public speaker, and author born on October 21, 1977.

She has a master’s degree in communication from New York University and is a New York Times best-selling author, as well as an Emmy Award winner.

Linsey has co-hosted segments of Good Morning America, 20/20, World News Tonight, Nightline, and News Live Prime.

Her published books include The Smallest Spot of a Dot, Stay This Way Forever, One Big Heart, The World is Awake, and How High is Heaven.
