JUNE McCamey's weight loss journey was chronicled during season 4 of My 600-Lb Life.

Fans of the hit TLC series want to know where June is now.

Who is June McCamey?

June was featured in season 4, episode 5 of My 600-Lb Life, which premiered on January 27, 2016 and is titled June's Story.

The emotional episode's description states: "At almost 600 pounds, June coped with the death of her son by overeating."

According to Screen Rant, June is " is one of the show's most incredible success stories," having made a great deal of progress after weighing 590 pounds when she first sought help from Dr. Younan Nowzaradan.

Where is June McCamey now?

Since her time on the hit reality television show, June has continued to make significant progress in her health and weight loss journey.

On July 4, 2019, she revealed that she had married her partner named Mary in an Instagram post.

As Screen Rant reported, June had set a goal to weigh 211 pounds prior to her wedding.

The outlet stated: "Though it was a lofty goal, June was dedicated to doing her best for her future wife, who had given up so much to support June."

While she did fully meet her goal, she was only off by 10 pounds, weighting in at 221 pounds before her nuptials.
