Who is Ralph Pittman from Real Housewives of Atlanta?

ENTREPRENEUR, producer, composer, advisor, and businessman are just some words to describe Ralph Pittman.

In February 2023, Pittman filed for divorce from Real Housewives of Atlanta star Drew Sidora after nine years of marriage.

Who is Ralph Pittman?

Ralph Pittman is the founder and CEO of My Mind Music.

He studied Business Economics and Music Theory at Rutgers University in New Jersey.

Pittman has also composed and produced music for My Mind Music for Kids, as well as for television and film productions.

According to its website, My Mind Music for Kids "is a sleep system curated to help kids reach REM sleep faster."

Are Drew Sidora and Ralph Pittman still together?

On Wednesday, March 1, 2023, PEOPLE reported that Pittman filed for divorce from Sidora.

Pittman told the publication: "Unfortunately we have made the difficult decision to move on to our next chapters, independent of each other."

According to PEOPLE, documents acquired by the publication state that Pittman's "petition for divorce" was received on Monday, February 27, 2023.

He stated that he and Sidora separated on Thursday, February 23.

The publication also notes that the documents said: "There is no chance or hope of reconciliation."

Before their separation, the couple reportedly went to marriage counseling to work through their issues.

The former couple wed in August 2014.
