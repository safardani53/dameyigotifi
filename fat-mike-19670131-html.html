Michael John Burkett, known by his stage name Fat Mike, is an American musician and producer. He is the bassist and lead vocalist for punk rock band NOFX and bassist for punk rock super cover band Me First and Gimme Gimmes. Fat Mike made his debut with his first band False Alarm in 1982. He credits Vandals Joe Escalante for introducing him to punk rock when he was 13 in summer camp.
