PARTY season might have already started, but there is still time to find beauty essentials to help you feel glam over the festive period.

In fact, shoppers are racing to Aldi after finding a dupe for the Dyson Supersonic hairdryer – and it is less than £15.

Over the last few years, the German supermarket has become the go-to destination for beauty dupes and influencers regularly raid the middle aisle for the latest finds.

From discounted candles to lip kits, you can find just about anything at Aldi but at a fraction of the branded product’s retail price.

With Christmas just around the corner, customers were already racing to the checkout to get their hands on dupes for the Le Vie est Belle and Black Opium designer perfumes.  

There is another reason for shoppers to visit the supermarket this weekend though, as Aldi have a dupe for the Dyson Supersonic hairdryer.

If you weren’t already sold, Tiktok channel @dontmesswiththedupes has raved about the hair styler.

Speaking about the product, the content creator revealed that it was ‘super lightweight’ just like the original Dyson design.

Thankfully though, the Aldi hairdryer doesn’t come with such a hefty price tag.
