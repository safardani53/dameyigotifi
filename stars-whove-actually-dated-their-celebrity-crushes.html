Stars Who've Actually Dated Their Celebrity Crushes

June 09, 2024

Jason Momoa, Olivia Munn and more have achieved the ultimate dream of transforming their celebrity crushes into real relationships.

Momoa met Lisa Bonet in the mid-2000s when they were introduced at a Los Angeles jazz club.

 “I mean, I didn’t tell her that [she was my celebrity crush],” he joked in a 2019 Esquire profile. “I didn’t let her know I was a stalker until after we had the kids.”

Momoa and Bonet, who share two children together, got married in 2017. They separated four years later in 2022.

Munn, for her part, was vocal about her interest in John Mulaney six years before they were romantically linked in May 2021 amid his divorce from Anna Marie Tendler.
