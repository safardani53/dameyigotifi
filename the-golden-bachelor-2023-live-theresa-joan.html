Faith’s ex-husband died in an at-home accident

Contestant Faith, who got tonight’s first impression rose, was married to her ex-husband, Brett Martin, for 20 years before they divorced.

Brett, a nuclear chemical operator, passed away at 61 years old at his home in Benton City on August 13, 2022.

The police arrived at the scene around 10pm after his family, who had been on the phone with him minutes before his death, called 9-1-1.

According to the coroner’s report exclusively obtained by The U.S. Sun, Brett “had been talking to some family members using Facetime when the fall occurred.”
