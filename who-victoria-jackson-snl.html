FAMED comedian Victoria Jackson rose to fame in the late 1980s after joining the cast of Saturday Night Live.

Since leaving the show, she has embarked on several other ventures in the entertainment industry, and now fans want to know more about her.

Who is Victoria Jackson?

Victoria Jackson is an actress, conservative comedian, and singer, who was born on August 2, 1959, in Miami, Florida.

She started her career in the early 1980s after starring in various TV shows and movies, but it was arguably her six-year stint on SNL that put her on the map.

Jackson starred as a cast member from 1986 to 1992 and was seen in over 100 episodes.

After leaving the show, she went on to have roles in Strip Mall, Shut Up and Kiss Me!, Her Minor Thing and Best Years Gone, among others.

To date, she has starred in over 75 productions, according to her IMDb profile.

When Jackson isn't on stage, she can often be found posting about her life on Instagram to over 34,000 followers.

Who has Victoria Jackson dated?

Since stepping into the spotlight, Jackson has been married twice and linked to several other high-profile guys.

Prior to her marriage to her current husband, Paul Wessell, whom she wed in 1992, she was married to film score composer and magician Nisan Mark Eventoff from 1984 to 1990.

She also had brief romances with Weird Al Yankovic and Andrew Dice Clay.

During Jackson's first marriage, she welcomed her first daughter, Scarlet, and then her second daughter, Aubrey, during her second marriage, making her a mom of two.

What is Victoria Jackson's net worth?

Due to Jackson's successful career, she has been able to amass a fortune over the years.

As of April 2023, she has an estimated net worth of $500,000, according to Celebrity Net Worth.
