Benefits of being verified on Ticketmaster

“Verified Fan flexes its muscle to ensure that more tickets go to the fans who will actually attend the event,” reads the company website.

Those with a verified membership are invited to purchase tickets for a show before their public release on a first-come, first-serve basis.

Ticketmaster adds: “Invitations are based on demand and ticket availability.”

Members who get invited will then receive a code via text message to access the early ticket sale.

They will then be placed into Ticketmaster’s Smart Queue virtual line, a system designed to keep ticket bots at bay.

How to get verified on Ticketmaster

Verified Fan is a service on Ticketmaster that offers members exclusive chances to score tickets for their favorite artists and venues.

Required for registration are an email address and a Ticketmaster account.

When an artist announces a show available for verified users, a link on the announcement will take buyers to the custom registration page.

Users are then asked to list the shows they are interested in seeing.

Ticketmaster will then send a confirmation email with instructions on what steps to take next.

Ticketmaster's ticket purchasing tips

Ticketmaster recommends the following when purchasing tickets:

Seat maps made available before presale

Fans have access to their preferred venue's seat map via Ticketmaster.

To more easily purchase tickets, it's recommended to take a look at the map and know what seats you want to grab before they're gone.

It's important to note that seat maps don't reflect the venue's availability.

Get to know the openers: Lawrence

Lawrence is a soul-pop band from the United States created by Clyde and Gracie Lawrence, from New York City.

The duo has been singing, performing, and creating music together since they were children.

Lawrence is now an eight-piece band with three studio albums, as well as multiple live and acoustic CDs.

Tour openers

The Jonas Brothers won't be traveling alone for their tour this year.

Opening up for the US and Canada is pop group Lawrence from New York City.

The group is fronted by fellow sibling duo Clyde and Gracie Lawrence.
