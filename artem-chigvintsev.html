Bio:
Artem Chigvintsev (born on June 12, 1982 in Izhevsk, Russia) is a Russian professional Latin dancer. He has competed on So You Think You Can Dance, and has returned as a guest choreographer on the show. He is currently competing on season 20 of Dancing With The Stars with partner Patti LaBelle.

Best Known For:
Artem Chigvintsev is best known for performing on the television shows So You Think You Can Dance and Dancing With The Stars.

Personal Life:

Chigvintsev has been married to fellow ballroom dancer Giselle Peacock, though they divorced in 2005. He has also dated dancer/choreographer Carrie Ann Inaba and actress Kara Tointon.
