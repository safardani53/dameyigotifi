What is Lord Alan Sugars net worth and is he married? The Sun

March 17, 2024

LORD Alan Sugar - formerly known as 'Sirallen' - is one of Britain's leading businessmen who most know and love from the BBC's The Apprentice.

Here's everything you need to know about the savvy investor and telly personality as he's back on our screens in the hit show...

What is Lord Sugar's net worth?

Alan Michael Sugar was born 24 March 1947, in Hackney, East London.

The Sunday Times Rich List estimated Lord Sugar's net worth at £1.21billion.

This makes him the 121st richest person in the UK.

Lord Sugar donates his fees for his work on The Apprentice to Great Ormond Street Hospital.

How did Lord Sugar earn his fortune?

The business mogul has earned his money through a string of business ventures, most notably founding Amstrad, a computer and electronics company, in 1968.

At the height of its success, Amstrad alone was worth an estimated £1.2billion, however, it was sold in 2007 to BSkyB for £125million.

More on Lord Sugar

On top of this, he runs Amsprop alongside his son, Daniel Sugar, who acts as MD for Amsprop Estates.

The company invests in commercial property across London and Essex and has since sold on some of their portfolios to other investors for a significant profit.

In 2015, the firm sold Burberry's Haymarket flagship store location on for more than double the price - cashing in a £65million cheque on a property that cost them £31.5million.

How did Lord Sugar become famous?

Lord Sugar first rose to mainstream attention as frontman of The Apprentice.

Prior to the now long-running show hitting our screens in 2005, Lord Sugar was not known beyond his business ventures.

As well as continuing to helm the show, he also fronted the spin-off programme Young Apprentice which ran from 2010-2012.

Lord Sugar has also appeared on Who Wants To Be A Millionaire and An Audience with Jeremy Beadle.

In January 2009 he was the subject of a BBC documetary hosted by Fiona Bruce, called The Real Sir Alan.

He has also presented the documentary Lord Sugar Tackles Football.

As well as appearing as himself in a cameo in Doctor Who and again in an EastEnders Children In Need special in 2012.

Is Lord Sugar married?

Alan is married to Ann Simons.

She is the aunt of former EastEnders star Rita Simons, on April 28, 1968.

The couple have three children together.

When was Lord Sugar chairman of Tottenham Hotspur FC?

Lord Sugar was chairman of Tottenham Hotspur from 1991 to 2001.

An avid supporter of the team, Sugar teamed up with Terry Venables to buy the club to help bail it out of financial troubles.

However, two years later Sugar sacked Venables and later won a legal battle against him when the former footballer appealed to the high courts.

The move was unpopular among fans, who felt he was treating the club as a business rather than a passion project.

In 2001, he sold the majority stake of his shares for an estimated £22million, before selling the rest six years later for £25million.

He branded his sixteen-year investment in the club as a "waste of his life".
