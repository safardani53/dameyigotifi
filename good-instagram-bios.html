50 Solid Instagram Bio Ideas to Spice Up Your Online Persona and Gain Followers

blog

There are some really good Instagram bios out there, and a good one is the first step to becoming an influencer. Everyone needs a good Instagram bio!

These days, everyone wants to be their own version of an influencer, and the first step to successfully gaining followers is having a good Instagram bio. An Instagram bio is like the cover of a book — it has to sell the product, and in this case, you’re the product! But we aren't all marketing gurus and geniuses, so how can we best sell ourselves?

Well, a good bio can make all the difference. Some people like to pick a Bible verse or favorite quote to show off who they are, while others simply reference their businesses or significant others. So, what are some examples of good Instagram bios to grab potential followers’ attention? Keep reading to find out!
