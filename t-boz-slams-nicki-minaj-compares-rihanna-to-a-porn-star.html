T-Boz SLAMS Nicki Minaj, Compares Rihanna to a Porn Star

September 23, 2024
blog

T-Boz sure knows how to promote her upcoming reality show.

With the singer set to anchor a program on TLC, T-Boz gave an interview to VH1 this week in which she made her feelings about a couple major artists VERY well-known.

First, although she doesn’t mention Rihanna by name, T-Boz certainly appears to tear apart the "Nobodies Business" crooner, saying on-air:

"Just because you’re on TV gyrating and boning and sucking penises, it’s not talent, honey. Maybe in the porn industry, but over here, we entertain.”

DIZZZAMN! How do we know Rihanna is the subject of that barb? Well, she does dance and pose rather provocatively. And, just moments later in the interview, T-Boz openly mocks the track "Umbrella."

 

Meanwhile, no guessing games are needed when it comes to this singer’s feelings on Nicki Minaj.

“How the hell is she gon’ tell me what I should be doing when her ass can’t even sing? Why is she there?" T-Boz said of the new American Idol judge.

"Now they’ve got rappers that don’t even sing judging. So, this is totally based off of popularity I guess. I mean, to each their own. Get your money, honey. All I’m saying is, I will never watch [your] shows again.”

Come on, T-Boz. How do you really feel?
