Billie Eilish is living her best life. See the chart-topper’s best photos of all time right here.

Billie Eilish is a force to be reckoned with in music. After emerging onto the scene with ‘Ocean Eyes’ in 2016, the singer-songwriter took over the radiowaves with her ear-worm ‘Bad Guy’ in 2019. She continued to break boundaries with her debut album, entitled ‘When We All Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go?’.

True to form, the singer cited some dark influences for the record. ‘Every song on the album is something that happens when you&rsquo;re asleep&mdash;sleep paralysis, night terrors, nightmares, lucid dreams,’ she told Zane Lowe. On top of that, the talented songstress performed at Coachella 2019 and won five Grammy Awards (including the coveted ‘Album of the Year’ honor) at the 2020 award show. She earned another major accolades in Mar. 2022 when she took home an Oscar for best original song for ‘No Time To Die’ from the James Bond film of the same name.
