12 Best Face Masks For Men 2020 | The Sun UK

March 30, 2024
blog

AS MEN'S skincare routines continue to evolve, face masks are becoming more of an essential cupboard item. You don't need to be a self-confessed metrosexual to be using a weekly mask to exfoliate, hydrate and improve your skin’s condition.

Masks are designed to supplement the daily routine of cleansing, applying sunscreen and moisturising, as well as deep-cleaning the face. We’ve picked out the best face masks for men that you can buy online, which fans rate highly and which consistently get top reviews.

A clay, mud or charcoal mask is the best way to deeply cleanse your skin once a week and ensure that you remove all dirt and impurities to brighten your complexion.

They work by absorbing dirt to leave skin looking more radiant, and with fewer blemishes.

You can also find cream or gel masks designed to target specific problem areas or skin issues, like masks with anti-ageing ingredients or those designed to fight acne.

For super-hydration, a sheet mask tends to do the trick and you may have noticed celebs from Chris Pratt to Cristiano Ronaldo wearing them on Insta: instead of rubbing onto the skin like a cream before washing off, you apply a sheet mask on your face, ensuring the cutouts are in the right place, and then you take it off after about 15 minutes.

US brand Golde has come up with a novel way to make face masks more appealing to men: the brand’s masks are packed with superfood ingredients like spirulina and lucuma to brighten skin - and they’re also edible (and reportedly very tasty).

If you’re feeling adventurous - and like the idea of a chemistry experiment in action on your face at any time - then try a bubble mask like Elizavecca Milky Piggy Carbonated Bubble Clay Mask, which fizzes and foams on the face. The bubble mask's formulation and ingredients means it reacts to oxygen exposure by bubbling and fizzing up.

We've rounded up the best face masks for men, including peel-off, sheet, black, clay and charcoal masks.
