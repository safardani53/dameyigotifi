Who is Rio Hackford's father, Taylor?

Born December 31, 1944, Taylor Hackford, 77, is known as an American film director.

Over the years, he has won a number of awards and worked on countless films.

He is arguably most famous for his work on Ray, Blood In, Blood Out, Dolores Claiborne, and An Officer and a Gentleman.
