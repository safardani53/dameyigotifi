Lana Del Rey New Album Cover Causes A Craze Among Her Devoted Fans

January 30, 2024
blog

Lana Del Rey has revealed the information for her new album, Did You Know There’s A Tunnel Under Ocean Blvd. The title tune and album cover art have also been published by the Summertime Sadness vocalist. The album’s title tune has lyrics by Del Rey and Mike Hermosa, as well as a video made by Del Rey, Jack Antonoff, Drew Erickson, and Zach Dawes. All collaborators, including producers Jack Antonoff, Drew Erickson, and Zack Dawes, are featured on the album’s cover art. It also features guests like Grammy-winning performer Jon Batiste, Father John Misty, Tommy Genesis, and engineer Laura Sisk, among others.

Lana Del Rey has already revealed that the album will be released on March 10, 2023. The album may be pre-ordered through the artist’s official website. Earlier this month, the singer announced the ninth studio album on Hola TV, expressing her excitement. Following the news, producer Jack Antonoff uploaded a snapshot of Del Rey on his Instagram account, implying that he will be engaged in her next endeavors.

Lana Del Rey’s new album cover has fans thrilled.

Lana Del Rey’s latest album elicited enthusiastic reactions from fans. Some even noted that the album covers are reminiscent of 1960s record covers.

More about Del Rey’s most recent work

Del Rey’s latest album Did You Know That There’s A Tunnel Under Ocean Blvd was published more than a year after her previous albums, Blue Banisters and Chemtrails Over The Country Club. Lana Del Rey just released Watercolor Eyes, a Euphoria song, and also covered John Misty’s Buddy’s Rendezvous. She also worked with Taylor Swift on the song “Snow on the Beach” off her new album. Swift said that she was a huge admirer of Lana Del Rey when describing the song.

Lana stated last month that her laptop, which contained vital work, had been taken. On Instagram Live, the singer described her trauma, writing:

“I parked my vehicle on Melrose Place [in Los Angeles] a few months ago and walked away for a minute, and the one time I left my bag inside my car, someone shattered all of the windows and stole everything.”

Several hard drives and camcorders, as well as her laptop, were taken from her vehicle, according to the singer. She said that her laptop included an unpublished novel she was working on.
