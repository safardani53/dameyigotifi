RAPPER Latto has dated a number of fellow hip-hop artists.

Here's everything you need to know about Latto's dating history.

Who has Latto dated?

Latto was rumored to be linked to a number of rappers including 21 Savage, Key Glock, Bandit Gang Marco, and others over the years.

21 Savage

Rumors that Latto was dating rapper 21 Savage have been circulating for quite some time.

In February 2021, it was reported that Latto and 21 Savage might have been linked when fans speculated that the rapper joined Latto for her 22nd birthday in Puerto Rico.

Eagle-eyed viewers noticed content similarities between Latto's videos on Instagram and Savage's friends' clips. They put two and two together to conclude that the pair may have vacationed there together.

“No,” Latto laughed when asked if she and the Savage Mode II rapper were a couple on Wild 94.1.

“It’s videos on YouTube of me performing with him at [Atlanta nightclub] Palace. It be real respect.”

The interviewer then probed about her 777 chain, as 7+7+7 equals 21.

She said: “As if I haven’t been saying 777. 777 is tatted on me."

The pair have collaborated on her song Pull Up which is featured on her first album, Queen of Da Souf. They also worked together on Latto's 2021 single, Wheelie.

In February 2023, Latto got a tattoo that says 'Sheyaa,' which could be an ode to Savage's real name, Shéyaa Bin Abraham-Joseph.

However, Latto has continuously denied rumors that she’s dating 21 Savage.

That same month, on TikTok, when a fan asked if the pair were dating, she responded, "No I've said I'm not plenty [of] times," according to Complex.

Key Glock

Latto was rumored to be romantically linked to Memphis' MC Key Glock back in 2020.

In an interview on the Big Facts Podcast, she claimed she did have a man but brushed off speculation that it was Key Glock.

“Nah. No,” she said when questioned about the rumor. “Nah, you see, my life be so public that if I can keep it lowkey, Imma keep it lowkey."

“This ain’t no like—clearly I’m not in the blogs and s*** like f***in’, PR as* relationship… I’m happy behind closed doors. I don’t want everybody in my business f***in’ that s*** up.”

“I’m not saying I’ll never tell the world,” she said. “I’m dead as* serious. I’m happy behind closed doors and I want to keep it like that.”

What has Latto said about her dating life?

In Cosmopolitan's July/August cover story, Latto told the publication that she is currently in "the longest relationship I’ve ever been in."

"If something is special to me, I'm going to keep it close to me because everything else has to be front and center about my life," she explained.

"I think we find the beauty in separation sometimes because it makes us miss each other. My privacy is more important [than seeing him in the crowd]. I can't have people putting two and two together."

When talking about trust, she told Cosmopolitan that she is so comfortable with her man that he has allowed her to be in her "soft girl era."

"I think people will probably be caught off guard or shocked at who I am in a relationship versus my public persona," she explained.

"Because people think I’m so tough because I’m super confident and dominant. In this relationship at least, I’m way more comfortable with taking the passenger seat and letting him lead because he lets me be myself—and I can still be my alpha self."

Who is Latto's mystery man?

Latto has remained quiet about her man's identity but previously told Atlanta radio station Hot 107.9 back in January 2023 that he pays her bills.

While fans search for clues, Latto explained to Cosmopolitan that she loves to cook and he loves to eat.
