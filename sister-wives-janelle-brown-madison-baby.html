SISTER Wives star Janelle Brown's daughter Madison has welcomed her third child.

Madison revealed her baby's name and debuted her first photo on Instagram.

"Welcome to the world Josephine Lee Brush!" 27-year-old Madison posted to her 532, 000 followers on Saturday.

The happy mom-of-three shared an adorable photo of Josephine wearing a pink babygrow and a matching hat.

Madison told fans "Little Miss Joey" was born Friday, February 10 weighing 8lbs 9oz.

Josephine is Madison's third child with her husband Caleb Brush, 36.

GROWING FAMILY

The Utah-native first announced she was expecting in July when she posted an image of Evie and Axel wearing "big sister" and "big brother" T-Shirts.

"The picture says it all!" she wrote. "The Brush family is growing by one early February."

In October, she showed off her matching bump with Christine's daughter Mykelti, who was pregnant with twins Archer and Ace at the time.

"It’s been fun being pregnant at the same time, sister!" she shared.

One week before giving birth, Madison wowed fans with her massive bump and wrote: "Everything is swollen and my hips are not loving me, but I am still feeling pretty good otherwise. Ready to welcome this little girl already!"

However, the expecting mom also admitted to feeling "anxious" during her pregnancy as her daughter Evie suffers from FATCO syndrome - a rare genetic disorder that causes limb malformation.
