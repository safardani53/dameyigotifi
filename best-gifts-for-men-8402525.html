The 68 Best Gifts for Men of 2024

April 18, 2024
blog

There are few clichés that ring quite as true as men being difficult to shop for. Of course, this depends on the person, but if you find yourself scratching your head this holiday season, look no further. We rounded up some of the best gifts for men, including must-haves from our 2023 Sexiest Man Alive, Patrick Dempsey, as well as skincare picks from John Legend’s Loved01 line, recovery tools from Tom Brady’s TB12 brand, and items we’ve spotted on celeb dads like Thomas Rhett and Drake. 

On top of celebrity dad picks (did we mention Michael Strahan and The Walking Dead's Jeffrey Dean Morgan recommended a few, too?), we’ve included some of our favorite PEOPLE Tested picks as well, so we’re confident your guy is going to get a lot of use out of anything on this list.

Read on for 68 gifts that are sure to be a hit this holiday season.

Under $50 Gifts

Yeti Rambler 10-Ounce Stackable Lowball

We can’t remember the last time we saw a photo where country music star Thomas Rhett wasn’t wearing a Yeti cap. Celebrities like Strahan and Drake are also fans of the brand, and in general, a Yeti rambler is a solid gift for anyone. We love this 10-ounce stackable lowball to keep your cold beverages ice cold, but if you're looking for more options, there are plenty of new releases on the brand's website, too. 

Bag Balm Vermont’s Original Skin Moisturizer

Many people suffer from dry hands in the winter — even our 2023 Sexiest Man Alive, Patrick Dempsey.

In our PEOPLE December 4th issue, Dempsey shares his favorite gifts under $150. His most affordable pick is this hydrating hand balm from Bag Balm. Dempsey says in the interview that he uses this when his hands start to get dry, “especially during the colder months.”

Sneaker Lab Shoe Cleaner

Dempsey tells PEOPLE that this is the best sneaker cleaner he’s found, adding that it “makes my old sneakers look new again.” If your guy obsesses over his sneakers, he will certainly appreciate this Dempsey-approved pick.

Philips Norelco OneBlade

We know it’s cliché to recommend a razor as a men’s gift, but when it’s the razor Dempsey uses, we think it’s important to share. “This gives me a close shave — and it’s travel-friendly, too,” he says in the interview.

Mark and Graham Nomad Rugged Case for AirPods

Apple Airpods Pro are a great gift, but if your giftee already has a pair (or if you’re looking for an under-$50 gift), then this customizable Airpods case from Mark & Graham is just what you need for a thoughtful, affordable gift he’ll actually use. 

Highever Magnetic Wristband Tool Belt

We love a clever Amazon find, and this magnetic wristband tops the list. If your guy is always completing a DIY project (or if you’d like him to start on a to-do list), this $17 find is a great gift to make the job a little easier.

Bombas Men's Solids Ankle Socks

A Michael Strahan favorite: Bombas Socks. Some people aren’t compelled to spend their own money on high-quality socks, and that’s exactly what makes them such a good gift. Plus, Bombas donates a pair of socks for every pair bought.

Roku Streaming Stick 4K

If a certain someone is difficult to shop for because they never stay in one place for very long, the Roku Streaming Stick 4K is an affordable, easy option that’ll please your travel-loving and Netflix-binging guy. Once his streaming apps are connected to the Roku, he can plug this stick into any smart TV in a hotel or Airbnb and rewatch The Office without interruption.

Filson Watch Cap Beanie

Channel your favorite Yellowstone cast member with Filson. The high-quality brand makes a plethora of Western-inspired work gear, but this affordable beanie would top off any outfit in an urban or country setting.

Artifact Uprising Walnut Desktop Photo Calendar

Want to remind your guy how much fun you’ve had together? This desktop calendar from Artifact Uprising is an easy way to showcase memories, and the high-quality feel of the product makes the $35 price a steal. 

Mark & Graham Corkcicle Cigar Glass

If your guy likes clever gifts, this customizable Corkcicle cigar glass is a fun gift to both give and receive. Monogram it with his initials and present him with this thoughtful yet affordable find that would be a great addition to any bar cart. 

FreshLetterStudio Dear Dad Personalized Journal

Though some of them might not like to admit it, there’s nothing quite as satisfying as finding a gift for a father figure who “didn’t want any gifts” and then making that gift a bit of a tear-jerker. This personalized “letters to dad” journal allows the gift-giver to fill the 150 pages with memories, letters, photos, and whatever else they want. It’s simple, affordable, and sure to become a keepsake for any dad of any age.

Carhartt Deluxe Lunch Cooler Bag

Practical and undeniably on trend, this Carhartt lunchbox will absolutely thrill anyone looking to transport multiple meals or a 12-pack of their favorite beverage. With over 33,000 5-star reviews on Amazon, it’s safe to say this will be a well-received present. The lunchbox comes in five colors, from camouflage to classic Carhartt brown, and at $25, it’s a steal.

Saxx Down Time Pant

It can be difficult to shop for clothes for men, but these buttery soft Saxx Down Time Pants make the task very simple. In fact, you’ll probably want to snag a pair for yourself after you get your hands on these. The pants scream cozy, yet they are super light and breathable, so if your guy runs hot, he’ll absolutely love these.

Loved01 Shave Cream

Yes, singer, songwriter, and producer John Legend has a skincare line, and we’re ready to spend our entire paycheck on it. Thankfully, it’s affordable, so we don’t have to be too budget-conscious. This Loved01 shaving cream is formulated for melanin-rich skin and is only $10, so you might well stock up. (Or combine it with the Exfoliating Cleanser, which model, author, and television personality Chrissy Teigen also loves.) 

Lodge 12-inch Cast Iron Grill Pan

If your dad loves his Lodge cast iron skillet, then add this Lodge version to his cooking set. Any home chef will appreciate this durable, flavor-inducing piece of equipment. 

Uncommon Goods Glass Topper Cocktail Smoker

If your loved one is constantly whipping up cocktails in the kitchen, up his mixology game with this fun cocktail smoker. It’s the one cocktail accessory that he almost certainly doesn’t have yet, and it’s fun to experiment with, too. You can purchase it either with or without the smoking chips or torch. Pair it with some nice liquor or a cocktail recipe book for a more complete gift.

Flowjo Dad & Me Activity Bucket List

Great for first-time dads or long-time dads, this little bucket list is as adorable as it is budget-friendly. This box set comes with 100 cards that feature creative, meaningful activities that encourage bonding and memory-making. It’s the type of gift that would be perfect to bring out for family vacations. It may only be $50, but it is sure to provide value for years to come.

SimpleSignCrafts Cool Uncles Club Sweatshirt

Uncle-themed merch can be kind of cheesy, but this one is undeniably cute, especially if the uncle in your life is in their 20s or early 30s. Everyone loves a new, cozy sweatshirt, and feeling like they’re appreciated as an uncle is an added bonus. It's a really affordable gift, so it’s hard to go wrong.

Tuff Mutt Hands-Free Bungee Leash

A hands-free leash may seem entirely unnecessary (hence why someone might be hesitant to buy it for themselves), but it can be a game-changing gift for a dog lover. Suddenly, that trip to the park seems easier. That stroll through the farmer’s market seems possible. It’s a great addition to daily walks, and for a nighttime stroll or jog, there's a reflective thread for extra safety.

Goldbelly Gift Card

When in doubt, give the gift of food. Goldbelly brings the best food from all over the nation right to your door. Purchase a gift card or choose from dishes like Central BBQ's Pulled Pork and Ribs or Nancy Silverton's Favorite Gelato Box to send to your treat-loving guy. Hopefully, he'll share.

Hydro Flask 24-Ounce Mug

Taking coffee on the go has never been easier with this reusable mug from Hydro Flask. The rubber-sealed lid prevents splashing and leaking, making it perfect for bumpy car rides. The stainless steel interior keeps drinks hot for hours, while the exterior is soft and comfortable to hold. Whether you’re shopping for someone who loves to camp or someone who just needs a new cup to take on their morning commute, this is sure to be a hit.

Anker Nano Power Bank

With as much as we’re all on our phones during the day, portable power banks are a lifesaver and make great gifts. This iPhone power bank is not only compact but powerful. Whether you know someone who loves to travel or uses their phone for battery-draining activities, this is a high-performing power bank that they’ll certainly get lots of use out of. 

Panel Sound USAPA Approved Fiberglass Pickleball Paddles Set of 2

Pickleball is all the rage right now with numerous celebrities (and Golden Bachelor contestants) getting into the fun and easy game. Whether you’re shopping for someone who already loves it or someone looking to get into it, this pickleball set from Panel Sound is the perfect gift. It features two rackets, four balls, and two cooling towels, all packaged nicely in a carrying case you can hold with the top handle or sling over your back.

Ursa Major Golden Hour Face Moisturizer

If your guy needs a good face moisturizer for dry skin in their skincare routine, this makes a great gift because it’s suitable for all skin types. Its plant-based formula and rich texture are perfect for improving fine lines, enhancing skin’s protective barrier, and reducing dryness. It’s the perfect addition to his (or your) everyday skincare regime.

Centr by Chris Hemsworth Massage Roller Stick

This massage roller stick from Chris Hemsworth’s fitness brand features a textured surface that tackles muscle soreness and tightness and can be used on his back, legs, or arms. This is a great gift for any athlete or fitness lover in your life so they can recover faster and get back to the gym. With each purchase, you also get three free months of access to Centr’s app, which features workouts and personalized coaching from a team of experts.  

iFixit Essential Electronics Toolkit

Some of the best gifts are the handy ones that you aren’t quite sure you’ll use initially but end up coming in handy later. This electronics toolkit is a perfect example of that. It features 21 pieces, like angled tweezers, and 16 high-precision screwdriver bits for easy fixes on hard-to-reach places. It’s all contained nicely in a magnetic case that’s easily stored out of sight when not in use. This is a great gift for any tech lovers in your life who prefer to fix their devices themselves.

Uncommon Goods College Cityscape Can-Shaped Glasses - Set of 2

Preserve a piece of his college memories with this university cityscape glass set. They have nearly 40 universities available to choose from, and each glass features a 360-degree design of iconic buildings from campus drawn in the college’s colors. As a nice touch, the college’s logo is stamped on the bottom of the glass. This can either be used as a festive cup for game time or kept as a fun keepsake.

Pendleton Whisky Belt Buckle

Made in partnership with premier jewelry brand Montana Silversmiths, this belt buckle features ornate floral detailing that surrounds the Pendleton logo and the year “2024” engraved on the back. This would be great as a keepsake gift for Christmas, Father’s Day, a birthday, an anniversary, or any other time you’re buying something special for a whisky lover in your life. If you really want to win "gift giver of the year" award, pair it with a bottle of the brand's premiere whisky, Director's Reserve.

Titleist Pro V1 Customizable Golf Balls

Tile Pro 2-Pack

Hush Puppies Men’s Earl Loafer

Truff Bestseller Pack
