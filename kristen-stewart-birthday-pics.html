Kristen Stewart will forever be known as the actress who brought Bella Swan to life on the big screen for ‘The Twilight Saga’ between 2008 and 2012. She especially shot to fame thanks to her real life romance with her on-screen lover, Robert Pattinson, who played dreamy vampire Edward Cullen.  They dated for much of the time ‘The Twilight Saga’ was filming, until Kristen was photographed kissing her married director from ‘Snow White and the Huntsman’ in 2012. Although that didn’t lead to an instant split, Robert and Bella officially parted ways by 2013. Since then, Kristen came out and has gone on to date stars like Soko, St. Vincent and Stella Maxwell. 

Now that ‘Twilight’ has already celebrated its 10th anniversary, its leading star’s career has come a long way. Deviating away from mainstream flicks like ‘Twilight,’ Kristen found success in indie productions like ‘Personal Shopper’ and ‘Certain Women.’ She’s also known for teaming up with Jesse Eisenberg for two films: ‘Adventureland’ (2008) and ‘Café Society’ (2016). After a string of these smaller but critically acclaimed films, Kristen landed a role as one of the three ‘Angels’ in the 2019 ‘Charlie’s Angels’ reboot.
