JONAH HILL is a comedian, actor, and filmmaker, known for his work in movies like Superbad and The Wolf of Wall Street.

At the December 5, 2021 premiere of his new film, Don't Look Up, Jonah wore a suit jacket with no shirt underneath, showing off his chest tattoos.

What is Jonah Hill's chest tattoo?

Jonah Hill has multiple tattoos across his chest and all over his body.

On his chest is a massive pirate ship, surrounded by a wolf head and a panther head.

According to sources close to the star, the ship tattoo was done by Bert Krak of Brooklyn’s Smith Street Tattoo Parlour in New York.

At Sunday's premiere, Jonah's tattoo was accentuated by a blue Gucci suit, which his girlfriend Sarah Brady also wore to match him.

When asked about his tattoos in a 2018 interview with Jimmy Kimmel, Jonah gave Bert Kerk a shoutout.

"I feel like I spent my whole 20s trying to be what people wanted me to be, and I didn’t know who I was," Jonah said to Kimmel about his tattoos.

"And the past couple years have been amazing, in the way of like, I’m just going to be myself."

His chest tattoo was first made noticeable in 2019, when the star was photographed while surfing.

Jonah has been photographed while surfing on multiple occasions, and opened up about his journey to self-acceptance of his body in February of 2021 after more photos were published of him shirtless.

"I'm 37 and finally love and accept myself. This isn't a 'good for me' post," he wrote at the time.

"And it’s definitely not a 'feel bad for me post'. It’s for the the kids who don’t take their shirt off at the pool. Have fun. You’re wonderful and awesome and perfect. All my love," Jonah added.

What is the movie Don't Look Up?

Don't Look Up is about two low-level astronomers who must go on a media tour to warn mankind of an approaching comet that will destroy planet Earth.

The ensemble cast features a plethora of familiar faces, including: Leonardo DiCaprio, Jennifer Lawrence, Jonah Hill, Meryl Streep, Ariana Grande, Timothee Chalamet, Chris Evans, Kid Cudi, Cate Blanchett, Tyler Perry, and more.

The film was written, co-produced, and directed by Adam McKay.

On December 10, 2021, Don't Look Up is set for a limited theatrical release, before premiering on televisions everywhere via Netflix on December 24, 2021.

At the premiere in New York on Sunday, December 5, Jennifer Lawrence showed off her baby bump alongside co-star DiCaprio, where fan-favorite scientist Bill Nye was also in attendance.
