Who is Joe Keery? 

Joe Keery is an actor who is most famously known for his role as the school bully turned redemption, Steve Harrington, in Netflix’s 2016 science fiction horror “Stranger Things.”

Keery is also known for his involvement in the psychedelic rock band, Post Animal, and his music, which he released under the name ‘Djo.

Early Life of Joe Keery

Joe Keery was born on 24 April 1992. Joe Keery is 30 years old as of 2022. His zodiac sign is Gemini.

He was born and brought up in Newburyport, Massachusetts, United States. Thus he holds an American nationality and belongs to the white ethnic group.

He was born and brought up in a Christian household and is a follower of Christianity himself. Joe Keery’s parents are David Keery and Nina Keery. David Keery, his father is an architect and his mother, Nina Keery, is a professor of English.

Joe has 4 siblings. He has 4 sisters out of which 1 is older and 3 are younger than him. The names of his sisters are Caroline, Lizzy, Kate, and Emma. Amongst these, Emma and Kate are twins.

Joe completed his initial schooling at River Valley Charter School which is a local Montessori elementary and middle school and later studied at Newburyport High School.

Joe completed his higher education at DePaul University, which is a Theater school, and later graduated from it with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Acting in the year 2014.

When he was young he also performed in an open theater at the Maudslay State Park, at a performing arts camp. He started acting in high school when his sister insisted.

Joe Keery Career 

Joe Keery started his career as an actor in the American Hollywood industry. In the year 2015, he did a small role of Gabe in the film ‘’Henry Gamble’s Birthday Party’’.

The same year he also starred in an episode of ‘Sirens’ as Scenester, 2 episodes of ‘Chicago Fire’ as Emmett, and an episode of ‘Empire’ as Tony Trichter III.

Joe was one of the guitarists of Post Animal, the Chicago-based garage and psych-rock band. Their 1st album was released in October 2015.

In his early 20’s he also released music under the name ‘Cool Cool Cool’.

In 2016, he starred in the movie ‘The Charnel House’ as Scott. From 2016 to date he starred as Steve Harrington in the famous series ‘Stranger Things’ in season 1 as a recurring role and in season 2 as the main role.

In the year 2017, he worked in the movie ‘’Molly’s Game’ as Trust Fund Cole.

In 2018, he worked in 2 movies titled ‘After Everything’ as Chris and ‘Slice’ as Jackson. In April 2018, he was the guitarist of Post Animal, he was part of the album ‘When I Think of You in a Castle’. However, he soon left the group after that.

In 2019, he worked in the short film titled ‘How to be Alone’ as Jack. The same year he also starred in an episode of the series ‘No Activity’.

On 19 July 2019, he released his 1st single titled ‘Roddy’ as a solo artist under Moniker Djo. On 9 August 2019, he released his 2nd single titled ‘Chateau (Feel Alright)’ under the moniker Djo again. On 13 September 2019, he released his debut album ‘Twenty Twenty’.

In 2020, he starred in the movie ‘Spree’ as Kurt Kunkle. The same year he also starred in the TV special ‘Death to 2020’ as Duke Goolies.

On 9 September 2020, he released a new single titled ‘Keep Your Head Up’. In the year 2021, shot for the movie ‘Free Guy’ as Keys.

For the year 2022, he has been working on some projects but he has desired a bit of a break from the industry as well.
