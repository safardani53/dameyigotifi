Who is Geoffrey Zakarian?

Geoffrey Zakarian was born on July 25, 1959, in Worcester, Massachusetts.

Geoffrey is a television personality, chef, restaurateur, and author.

He is also the executive chef of different restaurants in places such as New York, Miami, and Dubai.

He has featured prominently on several Food Network shows such as Chopped and The Next Iron Chef, and Iron Chef America.

He currently owns The National Bar & Dining Rooms in Manhattan, New York.

He also owns The National in Greenwich, Connecticut, and The National by Geoffrey Zakarian in Dubai, United Arab Emirates.

Geoffrey also has a consulting company, Zakarian Hospitality Consulting, which creates restaurant and bar concepts for clients looking to internally manage ongoing operations.

As of the time of writing, he consults for three places for The MacArthur Hotel in Sonoma, California - Layla restaurant, The Bar at MacArthur Place, and The Porch bar.
