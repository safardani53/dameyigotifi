Who is influencer Alexandra 'Xandra' Pohl?

October 06, 2024
blog

ALEXANDRA Pohl achieved internet fame when she started posting relatable content on her TikTok account.

The influencer would often share fashion and beauty videos, internet challenges, and snippets of her daily life online.

Who is influencer Alexandra Pohl?

Born on October 30, 2000, Alexandra Pohl, more popularly known as Xandra, is a social media influencer best known for her viral videos on TikTok.

The Ohio native is a graduate of the University of Miami, where she studied marketing and music business.

Xandra also works as a DJ in her spare time. She shared that she's been at it since she was a teenager.

"I've been DJing since I was a junior in high school, so I started off really early," the social media star told E! News.

"I just got really bored in high school with everything and I was like, what can I do?"

"I always loved music, music festivals, I was going to those since I was in grade school.

"And I was like, 'I really want to do music but I can't sing, I can't dance, I can't produce. What can I do?' I was like, 'I can mix.'"

After her mom got her a DJ board, she practiced until she broke into the frat scene and started playing at night clubs.

How did Alexandra Pohl get famous?

While Xandra started out as a DJ, what propelled her to fame were her TikTok posts.

Like her close pal and fellow influencer Alix Earle, she often shares "get ready with me" content, beauty tutorials, memes, dates with her boyfriend Jack Touzete, and nights out at parties.

Per Xandra, she never expected that her TikTok account would blow up.

"I'm constantly talking and doing whatever but I never expected people to listen," she told E! News.

"It was more for myself. TikTok was my little therapy session where I could talk and no one could say anything back."

She also doesn't hold anything back when it comes to her content.

"Honestly, for me, I like to be so unfiltered and real with everything that I'm doing.

"That's kind of my thing and no matter how I feel, it's going on the internet... It happens to everyone, so might as well be open with it."

As of this writing, Xandra has 1.1million followers on TikTok and 379k followers on Instagram.

How did Alexandra Pohl meet Alix Earle?

Xandra met fellow social media maven Alix during her freshman year at the University of Miami.

They've been inseparable since then.

"We've been best friends since before freshman year even started," Xandra divulged to E! News.

"It's just been such a crazy experience going through it together and having a best friend that's been there with me all the way through it and I've been there with her."
