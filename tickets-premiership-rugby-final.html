How to get tickets to the Premiership Rugby Final

May 11, 2024
blog

FANCY getting your hands on tickets to the Premiership Rugby Final? Us too.

The 20th anniversary of the Rugby final is sure to be a hit with sports fans and here's how you can be there.

  • Get tickets to the Premiership Rugby Final - click here

Available from ticketmaster.co.uk, now is the time to snap up tickets to the Premiership Rugby Final.

An unpredictable, unmissable, event the Premiership Rugby Final will be the first to be shown on TV this year to perhaps as many as 750,00.

And with the semi finals taking place on June 11, it's still all to play for - as the finalist will only be announced then after back to back rounds.

With competition heating up at the top of the Premiership between Leicester Tigers, Harlequins, Wasps and many more - make sure you get your tickets now.

Fingers crossed if you're a Harlequins fan, last season’s winners Harlequins have yet to lose a Premiership final. 

As well as the big game, there will also be entertainment from he Feeling and Sophie Ellis Bextor - so it's sure to be a huge day out.

How much are the tickets?

Adult Prices are: £99 (Category 1), £72 (Cat 2), £59 (Cat 3), and £39 (Cat 4)

Junior tickets are £20. These are only available at Categories 2, 3 and 4.

You can get tickets here from Ticketmaster.

Who's playing?

This will be confirmed after the semi-finals on June 11.
