GARDEN accessories can make all the difference to a green space, but often don't come cheap.

So it's little wonder that savvy shoppers are running to Aldi to nab a £9.99 mirror, that can help transform their gardens in an instant.

In fact, the mirror looks so luxe that people are even putting it inside their houses too.

Jayne took to the Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK group on Facebook to share a look at the mirror at the bottom of her stairs.

"Garden mirror but I've put it inside my house and it's looks beautiful," she wrote.

"£9.99 from Aldi they had other designs also."

And she wasn't the only person to have had the idea to put the mirror inside.

"Put mine on my landing," another wrote.

"Have 2 grey ones for the bedroom once I’ve finished decorating."

People were quick to comment on the post, with one writing: "Looks great, see others put two of these like side by side right in the corner of stairs/hall, one on each wall to open up the space, well done."

"I did this too! At the top of our stairs," another added.

"I bought the copper coloured one recently. They are a nice length," a third commented.

"Great ideas. Better than in the garden, as birds fly into them or attack themselves," someone else wrote.

But others insisted it looked brilliant in the garden, with one commenting: "I put mine in my garden much to the confusion of my partner who can't grasp why I'd have a mirror outside."

Another shared a picture of the mirror in their garden, with the addition making a huge difference to the space.

As well as getting mirrors from Aldi, others have nabbed similar ones from Home Bargains.
