Kim Kardashian on Appreciating Her Christmas Decorations

blog

Kim Kardashian’s extravagant holiday decorations never get old — even for the reality star herself! 

In a series of photos and videos posted to her Instagram Story on Sunday, the Kardashians star opened up about how much she loves marveling at the array of Christmas trees she has on display in her Los Angeles home. 

“This is truly the golden hour for me when my kids are asleep and I get to just appreciate all of my decorations and take some time for myself,” Kardashian says in one clip as she walks through the forest of snow-covered evergreens in her hallway. 

In another video, she shares the sparkling transition from her outdoor decorations into her cozy living area featuring a tall Christmas tree covered in white lights and more faux snow (a treatment called flocking).

“The trees outside being all lit and white, and then going into the white tree,” she adds as she pans around the living room before moving back into the hallway. “Going to the forest of trees here is just so cool.” 

The mom of four continues: “I’ll show you guys starting from the end just because this will never get old. You guys have no idea, these are all real trees so they smell so amazing and I just love the snow. It truly is heavenly.” 

In a close-up shot of the trees, she gives a shout-out to her family’s longtime florist, Jeff Leatham, for transforming her abode into a “true winter wonderland slice of heaven.”

The reality star also made sure to acknowledge her go-to event planner, Mindy Weiss, who had a hand in making her home look like a real-life snow globe. 

“Always there to see my simplistic vision out!” she wrote over another photo of her festive decor, tagging Leatham and Weiss in the caption. “I love you both! Thank you for the holiday magic.”

Kardashian is not the only person in her family who loves the wintry display. Her 10-year-old daughter, North West, was seen dancing and running through the forest of trees in a series of TikToks posted to the joint account that the mom and daughter share. 

Along with North, Kardashian shares daughter Chicago, 5, and sons Saint, 7, and Psalm, 4, with her ex-husband, rapper Kanye West.
