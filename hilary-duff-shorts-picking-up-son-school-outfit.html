“Hey moms! I came up with a semi cute outfit for school drop off! bleepzter claimed “You call this cute?!? A lady of class and substance wouldn’t be caught wearing this even in her worst hair day…” while andreacourtney15 noted,”Um sweetie you have no pants on…. Other than that totally down with the tips” OUCH!

But not everyone was a hater, as she had plenty of fans that had her back! bcolbert1978 worte, “I love the outfit!! As women we should empower, not insult. What is wrong with us? We need to move forward.” while erikalovesbunny noted, “Omg people. Do we really need to bash on someone who is clearly having a good day? She looks great BTW.”
