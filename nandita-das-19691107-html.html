Nandita Das is an Indian actress and director. She has appeared in over 40 feature films in ten different languages. Das has been recognized for many performances including in Fire, Earth, Bawandar, Kannathil Muthamittal, Azhagi, Kamli and Before The Rains. His first film, Firaaq, premiered at the Toronto Film Festival and went to more than 50 festivals, winning more than 20 awards. His second film as a director is Manto. Based on the life of 20th-century Indian-Pakistani short story writer Sadat Hasan Manto, the film was shown at the Cannes Film Festival in the category "Nothing worth talking about" In September 2019, Das was produces a two-minute clip for a public service announcement, India's Got Color. The clip addresses the issue of colorism and invites the audience to celebrate the diversity of Indian skin tones. His first book "Manto & I" recounts his six-year journey in making the film.
