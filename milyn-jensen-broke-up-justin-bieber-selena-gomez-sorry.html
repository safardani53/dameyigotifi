Milyn Jensen: Apologizing For Breaking Up Justin Bieber & Selena Gomez?

Milyn says she is stunned to be the reason Justin and Selena split up in a new interview with The Sun. “I was absolutely shocked when I read he had split up with her over what we did,” Milyn, who is appearing on the upcoming season of Bad Girls Club, says. “I hate being the girl that broke them up.”

Milyn sparked a huge controversy in January when she told Star that she and Justin hooked up in December 2012, while he and Selena were still together. “He was really kind and romantic,” the 22-year-old former nursing student said at the time. Justin “acted like he was [single],” she added.

That was enough to stop any ideas Justin and Selena might have had about reuniting, and the two have been on and off (but mostly off) ever since.

Milyn Jensen: ‘I’m On Team Selena!’

But now Milyn sounds like she’s sorry about exposing Justin’s alleged cheating ways — and she’s also 100% on Selena’s side!

In an exclusive interview with HollywoodLife.com, the Bad Girls Club star told us, “I’m a huge Selena fan and I’m on Team Selena! She is gorgeous! Anyone comparing me to her would be a compliment. I’ve gotten a few based on saying we look alike with our dark hair and skin!”

We’re not totally positive that Selena wants Justin’s alleged former mistress on her “team,” but it is interesting to see that Milyn has switched sides so fervently. A little more than six months ago she seemed hell-bent on breaking Justin and Selena up — now she apparently just wants everybody to be friends.
