My boyfriend is taxi-service for his out-of-work mate but won't drive ME anywhere

July 18, 2024
blog

DEAR DEIDRE: MY boyfriend says I’m being cheeky if I ask for a lift anywhere, yet his mate snaps his fingers and my fella goes running.

He used to take me out all the time and pick me up from work sometimes too, but now he says he’s too tired to drive me anywhere.

His mate is out of work but if he wants a lift home from the pub or he wants my boyfriend to go out for a poker night, they just jump in the car and go.

I’m getting fed up being stuck at home. My boyfriend is 19 and I’m 17.

DEIDRE SAYS: If you are not doing anything together and the relationship feels stale, perhaps it has simply run its course.

Relationships come and go in our teens. This isn’t just about lifts but about how low you are on your boyfriend’s list of priorities.

A good relationship is all about a connection. A walk in the park or a night in with a film and a pizza can be great – but if none of this is happening, perhaps you need to accept that it’s over and move on.
