Pics Of The Band Making Your Halloween A Scream Hollywood Life

February 12, 2024

Is “Thriller” failing to give you the chills? Does the pelvis thrust of “The Time Warp” fail to drive you insane? Will you turn green if you hear the “Monster Mash” one more time? Don’t be blue during the season of orange and black. LVCRFT (fka LOVECRAFT) is here. First arriving with ‘This Is Halloween Volume 1’ in 2019, and ‘The Sequel’ in 2020, the band gave a modern spin on some old horror music classics — and offered up their own take on spooky seasonal music. Their first album featured eleven blood-stained tracks of eerie electro ecstasy, like you’ve gone trick-or-treating with your ears. The group – featuring Lil Punkin, Deepkutz, Norman Crates, with a rotating cast that also includes Scary Ana Grande, Robyn Gothicke, Ghost M’lone, Count Trakula, and DejaVudu – kicked off the Halloween season in 2019 with their debut release. Lead single “Awooo” is the werewolf’s call that will get anyone howling at the moon, while tracks like “Skeleton Sam,” “First Blood” and “Take It To The Graveyard (Boo-Yeah!)” will get your bones to dance right out of your skin. The debut album also features two ghoulish, creepy cover songs: “Kim Wilde’s “Kids In America” and a blood-curdling rendition of Backstreet Boys’ “As Long As You Love Me” that will make you scream.
