Justin Hartley marriages: Who are his ex-wives?

March 06, 2024
blog

JUSTIN Hartley has been married three times - to Lindsay Korman-Hartley, Chrishell Stause, and now to Sofia Pernas.

It was revealed in May of 2021 that Hartley and Pernas have tied the knot.

Who are Justin Hartley's ex-wives?

Lindsay Korman-Hartley

Lindsay Korman-Hartley is an actress who was married to This Is Us star Justin Hartley from 2004 to 2012.

The former couple share joint custody of their daughter Isabella.

Lindsay was born in Palm Springs, California, on April 17, 1978, and is of Russian Jewish, Greek, and Italian descent, according to her IMDb page.

She was a national pre-teen talent winner of the Young Miss America Beauty Pageant and was cast in her first professional role at the age of 14.

The actress appeared on such soap operas as General Hospital, Days of Our Lives, and All My Children, and has 30 TV and movie credits on her resume.

Lindsay has kept her personal life out of the spotlight.

Chrishell Stause

Chrishell Stause is an actress and real estate agent who was married to Hartley for four years before their turbulent divorce in 2021.

Stause, who stars on the Netflix real estate reality show Selling Sunset, said she was blindsided by her husband's filing.

Hartley cited irreconcilable differences when he filed for divorce in November of 2020.

Stause broke down in tears in a trailer for Selling Sunset.

She told her friend Mary Fitzgerald: “I’m just kind of in shock with it all.”

“He just blindsided you?" she was asked.

Chrishell nodded.

"It's just a lot all at once because everybody in the whole world knows," she said.

“I love him so much. This was my best friend. Who do I talk to now?”

The couple married in 2017, three years after they began dating.
