February 12, 2024

PRIYANKA Chopra sent fans wild after posing in a see-through dress at a celebrity-filled bash amid her brother-in-law Joe Jonas' divorce drama.

The Bollywood actress attended the Victoria's Secret World Tour at The Manhatten Centre in New York while sporting a plunging black gown.

The star attended the red-carpet fashion show on Wednesday which also saw the likes of Gigi Hadid, Naomi Campbell, and Emily Ratajkowski.

Both Victoria's Secret veterans and Hollywood stars joined to celebrate the revamped runway show, which will air on Amazon Prime on September 26.

The re-imagined fashion show is described as "a celebration of women and global creatives, looking at fashion and art through a cinematic narrative lens," according to Parade.

Chopra was snapped wearing a black shimmery dress with a skin-bearing gap in the middle that sent fans on social media into a frenzy.

It comes as Chopra's brother-in-law Joe Jonas reportedly filed for divorce after allegedly catching his wife Sophie Turner on a Ring Doorbell camera.

Sophie and Joe’s four-year marriage is over and DNCE rocker Joe, 34, said their relationship was "irretrievably broken" in documents filed in the States.

It has not been made clear what Sophie was allegedly doing or saying in footage captured outside of their home.

But TMZ has reported that the alleged doorbell footage of Sophie was the “final straw” for Joe, who decided to end his marriage.

The iconic Victoria's Secret World Tour show - which broadcast annually on CBS and ABC for 20 years — was canceled in 2019 as the company worked to save its reputation during ongoing backlash over its lack of body diversity.
