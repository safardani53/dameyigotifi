Hayden Panettiere has opened up about her highs and lows in Hollywood. Take a look at her career in photos here.

Hayden Panettiere, born August 21, 1989, is an American actress who has been in the spotlight since she was a child. She first appeared on soap operas before a part in ‘Remember The Titans.’ Her breakout role was as Claire Bennett in the NBC superhero drama ‘Heroes.’ She also appeared in the movies ‘Bring It On: All or Nothing’, ‘Ice Princess,’ and ‘Scream 4’ before landing another big role with ‘Nashville’, opposite Connie Britton. The show lasted 6 seasons from 2012 to 2018.

Hayden previously dated ‘Heroes’ co-star Milo Ventimiglia from Dec. 2007 to Feb. 2009. She then dated Ukrainian pro boxer Wladimir Klitschko. They got engaged and had a daughter named Kaya together in 2014. They broke up for good in 2018, and then Hayden dated Brian Hickerson. He was arrested for assault and they are no longer together.

Hayden came clean about her own struggles with addiction and postpartum depression in a Jul. 2022 interview. She told ‘People’ that after the 2014 birth of her daughter, ‘I didn’t know where the alcoholism was ending and the postpartum was beginning, and I ran myself pretty ragged.’ Hayden has had many ups and downs over her career, including the death of her 28-year-old brother, Jansen Panettiere, in February 2023.
