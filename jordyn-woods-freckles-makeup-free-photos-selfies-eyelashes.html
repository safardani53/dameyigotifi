Jordyn Woods Shows Off Freckles &amp; Long Lashes In Makeup-Free Selfies Hollywood Life

September 29, 2024
blog

Jordyn Woods is known for her glam looks, but the 23-year-old actress and model stripped down in makeup-free selfies on Dec. 10. For some fans, it was their first time seeing Jordyn’s freckles!  “Are those natural freckles?? :),” one fan asked in the comments section, and Jordyn replied, “yes :).”

Aside than Jordyn’s freckles, fans also took notice of Jordyn’s exceptionally clear skin. Multiple fans demanded to know her “skin care routine” while raving over how much of a “natural beauty” she is. Jordyn also showed off her long and wispy eyelash extensions; it’s no surprise that Jordyn has perfect lashes, considering that she has her very own false eyelash line with Eylure!

“Frecklesssss,” Jordyn wrote underneath the natural selfies, and even teased an exciting project in her caption. “About to film a little YouTube video for you guys! Can you guess what it is?,” Jordyn added. Jordyn has recently resumed uploading videos to her YouTube channel, which now has 330,000 subscribers.

Jordyn’s last YouTube video, which she uploaded on Dec. 5, was a vlog that starred her new boyfriend Karl-Anthony Towns. Jordyn confirmed her romance with the 25-year-old Minnesota Timberwolves player in September, amid her 23rd birthday trip in Mexico. Before they were lovers, Jordyn and Karl were friends, and grew close in the wake of Karl’s mom’s passing this past April. In this most recent YouTube video, Jordyn and Karl were traveling to Florida for Jordyn to make a cameo in a friend’s music video. She has been appearing in all kinds of exciting projects lately, which included competing as the “Kangaroo” on Season 3 of The Masked Singer. As you can see, Jordyn is focused on herself — not the drama surrounding her, like Larsa Pippen‘s fallout with the Kardashians, whom Jordyn also cut ties with in 2019.

“I’m not really involved in that,” Jordyn said on PrettyLittleThing’s Behind Closed Doors podcast in regards to the tensions between Larsa and the Kardashians. Jordyn added, “I’m all about moving forward and going forward so I wish everyone the best. I’m not really sure what’s going on. I’m just focused on myself and my well-being.”
