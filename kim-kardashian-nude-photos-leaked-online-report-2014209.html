Kim Kardashian Nude Photos Leaked Online: Report

June 30, 2024
blog

Yet another celeb has apparently suffered a nude photo leak. Naked photos of Kim Kardashian were allegedly leaked online recently, TMZ reported on Saturday, Sept. 20.

The racy snapshots of Kanye West’s wife were published on Reddit and the thread 4chan, but were quickly taken down. According to TMZ, the star wasn’t wearing her wedding ring at the time. It appears a hacker also posted nude pictures of Vanessa Hudgens, Olympic soccer player Hope Solo, Lake Bell, Avril Lavigne and Leelee Sobieski.

Kardashian famously became a household name when a sex tape she filmed in 2003 with then-boyfriend Ray J was leaked in 2007. While discussing her marriage to West in a new interview with British GQ, the Keeping Up With the Kardashians star admitted she doesn’t want to “make the same mistake twice.”

“My husband Kanye and I have an amazing sex life,” Kardashian said. “So far as the sex tape is concerned, whether or not we’ve made another…If we have, it’s never something I want to go public.”
