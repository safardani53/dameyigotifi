Gerry Turner and Theresa Nist are getting married! After their engagement on ‘The Golden Bachelor,’ the couple tied the knot in ‘The Golden Wedding’ special. See all the photos from the Bachelor Nation event.

The Golden Wedding was an unprecedented Bachelor Nation event. Gerry Turner and Theresa Nist said “I do” at a gorgeous wedding in Palm Springs, California, on January 4. Their wedding was televised live in The Golden Wedding special.

Gerry and Theresa’s nuptials featured many familiar faces from Bachelor Nation, including Trista and Ryan Sutter, Charity Lawson, Leslie Fhima, Joey Graziadei, and more. Scroll down to see all the photos from The Golden Wedding event.
