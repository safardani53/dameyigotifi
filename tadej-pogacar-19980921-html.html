Tadej Pogačar is a Slovenian cyclist currently driving for UCI WorldTeam UAE Team Emirates. He became the youngest cyclist to win the UCI World Tour race, winning the Tour of California 2019 at the age of 20. On his Grand Tour debut, Pogačar grabbed the three races of Vuelta a España 2019 en route to third place. On his debut at the Tour de France, he won three races and won the final race. At Tour de France 2020, he achieved the general classification, classifying young racers and mountain classifications, thus becoming the only driver to win simultaneously these three categories. The win was the result of a historic performance in the penultimate individual time test in which he became the first racer since Eddy Merckx in 1972 to win three special jerseys. He was the first Slovenian winner and at the age of 21 he became the second youngest winner behind Henri Cornet, the winner in 1904. He currently occupies second place in the UCI world rankings, behind fellow countryman Primož Roglič.
