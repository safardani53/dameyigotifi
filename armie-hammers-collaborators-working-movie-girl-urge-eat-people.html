ARMIE Hammer's Call Me By Your Name collaborators are working on a new movie about a girl with the "urge to eat people."

The new project comes as the 34-year-old actor is under fire following his "cannibal" sex texts scandal.

Timothée Chalamet and Luca Guadagnino, who worked together on the 2017 film with Armie, are planning a reunion with the upcoming the coming-of-age horror story Bones & All.

Taylor Russell, 26, is in talks to star in the movie, which will be directed by Luca, 49, and co-star Timothée, 25.

The project does not yet have a distributor, though filmmakers are hoping to begin working in April or May.

Based on the novel of the same name by Camille DeAngelis, the film will follow Maren Yearly as she ventures on a cross-country trip in search of the father she's never met.

Throughout the journey, Maren will attempt to understand why she has the urge to kill and eat the people that love her.
