1000-Lb. Sisters fans pray for a 'speedy recovery' as star reveals she's been left 'sore &amp; bruised'

1000-LB. Sisters star has Amanda Halterman received tons of fan support after opening up about her terrible car accident.

Amanda, 42 - the half-sister of Tammy and Amy Slaton - shared a frightening image from her incident. 

In the Instagram photo, the blue car appeared damaged with a flat tire, a dented passenger door, and scratched windows.

In her lengthy caption, the 1000-Lb. Sisters star thanked the EMTs and the first responders for "everything they always do."

She continued: "Special thanks to Mr. Bunner and Lisa and a huge special thank you to a person who wants to remain anonymous."

Amanda closed: "Thank you, God, for keeping me, and the person who hit me, safe.

ANOTHER SEASON?

Recently, Amanda answered a fan question in her latest TikTok video.

In a dimly lit room, the TLC alum vaguely revealed whether or not there will be another season of her family show. 

Because she signed a non-disclosure agreement with the network, she cannot discuss the future of the series.

Amanda went on: "Unfortunately, I'm not allowed to talk about the question that you're referring to: Are you gonna get another season?

"I signed a non-disclosure agreement, and I cannot disclose that information at this time, but I can tell you that I appreciate your support. So, appreciate you."

Throughout the video, Amanda showed off her infinity symbol and the heart tattoo on her chest.
