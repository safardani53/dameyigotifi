Inside Kendall Jenners private $2.3M car collection with a $220K 1960 Cadillac

January 30, 2024
blog

KENDALL Jenner has an extensive car collection that landed her guest appearances on car-themed shows like Jay Leno's garage.

Here’s your inside look at some of the vehicles that this reality TV star has been known to drive.

Cadillac Eldorado

Kendall’s purple 1960 Cadillac Eldorado reflects her passion for vintage automobiles.

The model acquired her classic convertible Cadillac in 2017, and the car is estimated to be worth $220,000 AutoBizz reports.

Chevrolet Corvette

Kendall’s episode of Jay Leno’s garage showcased her light blue and white 1957 Chevrolet Corvette that was restored by a member of the National Corvette Society.

Hagerty reports that the Corvette’s estimated value is around $127,000 when in “excellent” condition.

Chevrolet Camaro

Another one of Kendall’s classic rides is a $500,000 1969 Camaro with a triple-black design striking an ideal balance between being eye-catching and casual.

These blacked-out components include the Camaro’s wheels and taillights, while an automatic top helps this multimillionaire enjoy Southern California weather.

Rolls-Royce Dawn

Kendall’s Rolls-Royce Dawn is a $356,000 whip with a 6.6-liter V12 delivering 563 horsepower.

In November of 2019, the model took her Rolls-Royce out to a track to learn how to drift.

Mercedes-AMG G-Class

This media personality owns a Mercedes-AMG wagon, but didn’t get caught crashing the SUV after her 16th birthday like her sister Kylie.

Kendall’s G-Wagon is a $112,000 G-500 Cabriolet that was one of 200 units manufactured in 2012 and 2013, Autoevolution reports.

Ferrari SF90 Stradale, 488 GTB Spyder

Kendall owns Ferrari’s first hybrid, the SF90 Stradale, which starts at $516,000.

Ferrari’s SF90 Stradale produces a jaw-dropping 986 horsepower through its twin-turbocharged V8 and electric motor supplying an additional 217 horsepower.

This reality television star’s other Ferrari is a 488 GTB convertible with a $249,150 base price.

The 488 goes from zero to 60mph in 3 seconds flat and has a top speed of 203mph.

Lamborghini Urus

Kendell Jenner was shown pulling up in her brand-new $260,676 Lamborghini Urus in Hulu’s new series, The Kardashians.

Autoevolution reports that Kendall’s Urus was purchased from the high-end auto dealership, Banned Auto Group, in Van Nuys, California.

Key specs include within this Lamborghini Urus include:

  • A 641 horsepower 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8
  • 0-60 mph time of 3.6 seconds
  • 190 mph maximum speed
  • A matte brown paint job

Fourth-Generation Range Rover

Kendall received a Range Rover for her 16th birthday and has continues to love the Land Rover brand today.

The socialite currently drives a fourth-generation Range Rover with a starting price of around $95,000.

Land Rover Defender 110

This multimillionaire was spotted buying her Land Rover Defender 110 in Sherman Oaks, California, in April.

A new Land Rover Defender 110 has a sticker price of $114,600, off-road capability and three rows of seating.
